Bookshelf

I love reading books and enjoy browsing through others' reading lists and bookshelves to see what they're reading. This is why I'm sharing what is basically my digital bookshelf on here. These are some of the best books I've read over the past years. The list is obviously non-exhaustive and is bound to change and develop. I've no doubt missed some great books on here and I am always open to book recommendations. All books displayed here are not in any specific order. In other words, the list is as jumbled as any regular bookshelf.

Got recommendations? Let me know
